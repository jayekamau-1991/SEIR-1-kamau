QUESTION: What does identity control in a cloud system?

---

PART 1: Authentication, Authorization, and Trust
-------------------------------------------------

Authentication is about proving who you are.

Authorization is about what actions I am permitted to perform on what resources.

Trust is a configured relationship between two systems.
GCP trusts Entra ID to verify users.

---

PART 2: Why a Lockout Is an Outage
------------------------------------

A server outage has a known recovery path — restart it, roll it back, redeploy.
The tools to fix it are still accessible.

If the admin account is locked out, the person who needs to fix the problem
cannot get in to fix it. The infrastructure is still running but no one
authorized can touch it.

---

PART 3: Why Access Recovery Is Harder Than Deployment Rollback
---------------------------------------------------------------

Deployment rollback will automatically restore the files.

Identity recovery requires using a trusted account to access it and there must
be a person — a human — that is available to execute the fix to get things back
in the right order.

If it happens that the trusted account was also impacted, the recovery chain is
broken and now we need to escalate even further.

One bad step might exacerbate the lockout instead of fixing it.
